Biography

I am an Associate Research Fellow in the Faculty of Artificial Intelligence in Education at Central China Normal University, where I am a member of the Intelligent Education Team led by Prof. Sannvya Liu . I received my Ph.D. from the College of Computer Science and Technology at the National University of Defense Technology in 2026, under the supervision of Prof. Xinwang Liu. Before that, I received my M.S. from the School of Computer Science at China University of Geosciences (Wuhan) in 2023, under the supervision of Prof. Chang Tang.

My research interests include multimodal learning and unsupervised learning.
